Sterilization Process

Carefully controlled stages designed for effective, reliable sterilization.

Start

Packaging

Products are sealed in breathable materials.

Preconditioning

Temperature and humidity are optimized.

Gas Exposure

EtO gas sterilizes under controlled conditions.

Degassing

Gas is safely removed through wash cycles.

Finish

Aeration

Residual gas is reduced to safe levels.

Why Our Process Matters

Ethylene Oxide sterilization is especially valuable for products that cannot tolerate heat, moisture, or radiation. Our process is designed to protect product integrity while achieving effective microbial elimination.
